ABOUT US

Hello my name is Abubeker Adem and I'm the founder of bale mountains hiking tour. I was born and live in small town  DINSHO,  where the Head quarter of the park suited. Being born and live in wilderness and interracting with it in every day action can make anyone fall in love with it. My main objective is starting this company was to promote local tourism and introduce more people to the beauty of bale mountains national park and Ethiopia as a whole. I want to provide clients with a better level of service and an overall unforgettable experience.i believe that through this, I can help make Ethiopia world class tourist destination. I have been working on tourism service and tourist guide in bale mountains and in whole Ethiopian country for 18 years and as well as in botanical researchers those needs to plant identification more than ten years. With those students from higher education institution in the country side as well as from different corners of abroad diversities and research centers concerning investigation of plant types and identifications.
