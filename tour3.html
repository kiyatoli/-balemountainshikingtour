Three Days and
Two Nights

BALE MOUNTAINS WILDLIFE TOURS

Days

1

-Arrival at Bale Mountains National Park.

Hiking from Dinsho to Sodota campsite/habera 20/14km After arrival at bale robe airport will took you with car then driving 30 km to Dinsho national park headquarter then start hiking, this day will be hiking through the village and with stunning landscape then enter the national park, there is a chance to see the rarest canid species Ethiopian wolf, rock hyrax, rodent species and variety of bird species like birds of prey and stop by habera waterfall then heading for the nice breathtaking then to SODOTA campsite for overnight at altitude of 3520m a.s.l and if spent overnight at habera campsite at altitude of 3480m a.s.l .

2

Hiking from Sodota/Habera to Adele campsite 17/14km

-Today after having breakfast heading to Adele through web valley,on the way there is a chance to see the Ethiopian wolfs, serval cat, rodent species like gaint mole rat Wich is endemic to the national park and other endemic bird species then crossing the heaterland forest and there is availabilities to spot the antelope species such as mountain nyala, menelik's bushbuck, bohor reedbuck, grey duicker and also spotted hyena after arrived at Adele campsite for overnight in the forest at altitude of 3300m a.s.l

3

Hike down from Adele to Dinsho 12km

Morning after having breakfast hike down to Dinsho for about two hours, on this morning passing through hygenia and juniper wood land trees,on the way we will spot animal like colobus monkey, olive baboon, menilik's bushbuck, mountain nyala, bohor reedbuck, warthog, grey duicker and there is numerous bird species on the way, then meet with car to transfer you to the airport for flight as per your plan.
