ABOUT THE FOUNDER

Hello, my name is Abubeker Adem, and I'm the founder of Bale Mountains Hiking Tour. I was born and live in the small town of Dinsho, where the headquarters of the park is located. Being born and living in the wilderness and interacting with it in every daily action can make anyone fall in love with it. My main objective in starting this company was to promote local tourism and introduce more people to the beauty of Bale Mountains National Park and Ethiopia as a whole. I want to provide clients with a better level of service and an overall unforgettable experience. I believe that through this, I can help make Ethiopia a world-class tourist destination. I have been working in tourism services and as a tourist guide in Bale Mountains and throughout Ethiopia for 18 years, as well as with botanical researchers who need plant identification for more than ten years. I have worked with students from higher education institutions in the countryside, as well as from various international diversities and research centers, concerning the investigation of plant types and identifications.
